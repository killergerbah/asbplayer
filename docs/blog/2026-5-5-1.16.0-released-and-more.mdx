Lots of stuff has happened:

- Extension v1.16.0 was released just now.
- Extension v1.15.0 was released almost a couple months ago, but I forgot to post about it.
- asbplayer now belongs to its own [GitHub org](https://github.com/asbplayer).

<!-- truncate -->

I'd like to acknowledge the first-time contributors in this release:

- [@jprostko](https://github.com/jprostko) added subtitle detection support for SVT Play and UR Play.
- [@AkihaZhang](https://github.com/AkihaZhang) greatly improved and extended the Simplified Chinese localization of asbplayer.
- [@xwxb](https://github.com/xwxb) added online subtitle support via the Jimaku API.
- [@yqmmm](https://github.com/yqmmm) fixed POT usage in YouTube subtitle detection.

Read the full release notes for v1.16.0 [here](https://github.com/asbplayer/asbplayer/releases/tag/v1.16.0).
